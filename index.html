CASCADIA WE ENGINEERS CLUB

Welcome to the Cascadia WE Engineers Site! This site is the project of its current members! More about us: We are a passionate group of Computer Science students at Cascadia College.

About the Club

We hope to inspire students to pursue their dreams of studying or working
within a STEM-related field, and apply themselves
passionately to fully unleashing their potential in the engineering world.

We are meeting virtually through Zoom every Monday, 1:30-2:30pm. You can also reach out through Instagram or stay up to date through Discord!

Sound interesting?

If you have an interest in the coding, hacking, learning new things, or just looking for a community to be a part of, feel free to email us at CascadiaWE@gmail.com
